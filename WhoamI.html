Lily Pellaud (*2000) is a Swiss artist based in Zurich (CH) Lily is working mainly with site-specific and immersive installations. After completing her bachelor's degree at ZHdK, she is currently an internationally exhibited artist whose works have been shown in Indonesia, the UK and Switzerland. Her latest project titled "Untitled (and overworked)" was recently presented at NEST space (ZH).She also as been an active member of the Collective 2.5 since 2021.
