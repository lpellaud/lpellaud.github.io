Lily Pellaud (*2000) is a Swiss artist based in Zurich (CH).

Pellaud is working mainly with site-specific and immersive installations.
After completing her bachelor's degree at ZHdK last year, she is currently an
internationally exhibited artist whose works have been shown in
Indonesia, the UK, and Switzerland. Her latest project, in collaboration with Oleksandra Tsapko,
titled "Heivi in the mountains", received great critics from the board of the IFCAR Grant.
Pellaud has also been an active member of the Collective 2.5 since 2021.
